Quota Means "We Share"

The Quota Andover chapter is dedicated to help those in need. We support disadvantaged women and the hearing impaired. This group supports people in need by receiving donations and using fundraising events to obtain money. The hearing impeared costs can be anywhere from $2,000 - $3,000 and they need your help. Our non-profit organization is here to help the Andover community.

Child Issues

Quota International believes that all children should have access to health care, education, daily nutritious meals, and a sense of security. Children are extremely powerless to threats in society. It is society's job to make sure these threats do not affect their well-being and development. By securing our children, we are securing the future of our world. The following programs ensure that children's most basic needs are met: Nutritious Meals, Cops n' Kids/QuoCKa Literacy Program, Health Care, Screenings, and Check Ups, Hospital Diagnostic Equipment, and Teddy Bear Distribution For Children In Trauma. These programs will ensure the development of our children.
